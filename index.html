Gryphon Foundation for People with Disadvantages

Serving People with all types of disabilities, and their caregivers. We strive to help anybody experiencing socio-economic hardship.

The Mission?
Raise Awareness, Improve Lives

We are focused on improving the quality of life of people dealing with significant disadvantages. We promote independent living and universal access at public spaces.

Any funds raised will be split into 2 groups at a 3:1 ratio.

1. Intelligent Power Wheelchair (IPW) R&D, including student internships, and travel related to IPW public demos. This includes loan payments for the Independent Living Recreational Vehicle (ILRV).

The IPW will be used to film a unique documentary from the perspective of a power wheelchair user.

2. Support for business operations including this website, email, and a wheelchair accessible van.
